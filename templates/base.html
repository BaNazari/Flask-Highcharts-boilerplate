I tell you about the charts.

The data is presented as recorded amounts of daily walked distance for 9 healthy male persons in each day of the year. This is in fact based on a random generated CSV file, so there would not be a trend in data. This is the first seed for the visualization tool which I am developing. More parts and features will be added gradually.


You can:

  • Turn the charts in "Line chart" on or off by clicking the names on the legend part.
  • Zoom in both charts by draggin your mouse horizontally on charts.
